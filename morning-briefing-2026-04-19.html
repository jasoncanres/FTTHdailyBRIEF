XGS-PON now default for new FTTH builds through 2030
20/22
Tech/Strategy 🔴 Act Today Available Now
What's emerging XGS-PON (10G symmetric) is the mass-market upgrade standard. 25G PON and 50G PON exist as targeted overlays but remain specialized through 2030. XGS-PON reuses existing fiber plant — capex only on OLT/ONT when customer upgrades.
Why it matters for growth Specifying GPON on any new 2026 build is a 5-year capex regret. XGS-PON is the minimum viable architecture to compete on 2-gig+ tiers against AT&T/Verizon mobile bundles.
How to apply Deploy now. Mandate XGS-PON on every new design spec. Reason: customer upgrade path is free (no re-trenching), and it eliminates the "we need to rebuild to compete" decision two years out. Line up dual OLT vendors (Nokia + Adtran or Calix) to protect against single-vendor pricing.
