Policy / Regulatory

MDU Fiber Activation: Cost-Effective Growth from Existing Infrastructure
13/22 🟢 Queue It
Technology Available Now

Industry analysis highlights MDUs as a high-ROI growth vector: millions of potential subscribers live in buildings where fiber passes nearby but isn't fully utilized. By tapping existing in-building wiring and simplifying installation, operators convert these buildings into active fiber homes at a fraction of greenfield per-sub cost.

Why It Matters for Growth

MDU activation dramatically improves passings-to-subscriber conversion — the fiber is already in the ground, so customer acquisition cost drops and time-to-revenue compresses.

How to Apply It

Pilot test. Audit your existing fiber routes for MDU proximity. Identify the 10 highest-density MDU clusters within 500 feet of your plant and model the economics of inside wiring vs. greenfield extension. If IRR exceeds your threshold, launch an MDU-first growth initiative in Q3.
