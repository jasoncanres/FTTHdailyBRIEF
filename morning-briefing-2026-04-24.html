MDU "near-ready" conversion — existing in-building wiring 19/22

🔴 Act Today Available Now

What's emerging

Genexis (FTTH Conference 2026 demo) and Calix published solutions that deliver fibre-like speeds over existing in-building copper/coax in MDUs with fiber to the basement. Dramatically cuts cost per door vs. full rewire and bypasses landlord-permission bottlenecks.

Why it matters for growth

MDUs are where take rates are highest and install cost per door is lowest — ignoring this is leaving the easiest subscribers on the table.

How to apply it

Deploy now. Identify your top 20 MDU properties within 500 ft of existing backbone in Bluffton/Hilton Head/Savannah markets. Pilot Genexis or Calix in-building solution on two buildings this quarter. Why: the entire Southeast coast is disproportionately MDU-heavy vs. national mix.

Open-access / wholesale NetCo model accelerates 19/22

🟡 This Week Early Deployment

What's emerging

Infrastructure capital is aggressively pushing the NetCo/ServCo separation model — network owner sells wholesale access to multiple ISPs. CoBank reports and deal activity show wholesale-first fiber now a credible US thesis, not just European. Anchor-tenant commitments de-risk the economics from day one.

Why it matters for growth

Unlocks infrastructure-grade capital (lower cost, longer duration) and converts take-rate risk to multiple-ISP demand aggregation.

How to apply it

Add to roadmap. Model an open-access variant of your next build — two ServCos committed before ground-break, NetCo margin target 55%+ EBITDA. Why: separates capital-raise from operating execution, which opens infra-fund financing at 100–200bps lower cost of capital.
