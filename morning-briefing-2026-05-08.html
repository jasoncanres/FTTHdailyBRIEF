3. Technology & Growth Strategy

XGS-PON now default; 25G-PON for premium and competitive defense↗
18/22🟡 This Week
Available Now
What's emerging
Symmetrical 10G PON (XGS-PON) reaches 55% of the PON market in 2026, displacing GPON in all serious greenfield. 25GS-PON is in early deployment among major cable MSOs as a residential/SMB premium tier and as a competitive moat in markets they want to defend from overbuilds.
Why it matters for growth
XGS-PON is now table stakes — without it, your wholesale customers and converged-services pitch fall flat. 25G-capable OLTs cost ~10–15% more than XGS-only chassis but eliminate a forklift upgrade in 5–7 years.
How to apply it
Deploy now: standardize all new builds on XGS-PON with 25G-capable OLTs (Nokia Lightspan FX, Calix Intelligent Access). Don't deploy 25G ONTs at scale yet — keep them as a premium tier in MDU/SMB. The capex premium is cheap insurance.
